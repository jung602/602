2021 Membership Emergence Project is an exhibition of stories which shines light on the thoughts of members of Samsung Design Membership. In MEP , the members of Samsung Design Membership not only discover the other side of an object, but also explores on the true value they carry. In the process of exploration, the light shines on the thoughts and thoughts hold the light. We hope that the thoughts holding the light exhibited in the 2021 MEP will bloom as another light for those who look at us.

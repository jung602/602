Short-form videos are now the mainstream content of Gen-Z culture. The biggest success factor of short-form videos is the 'Derivative Creation' culture that can imitate and derive the original freely. Vibop is a new short-form platform that reflects derivative creative culture in UI and uses NFT to protect creators' rights.

BACKGROUND

Short-form video platforms are growing rapidly. In the background of growth, there are 2 reasons. First, the short video length fits the mobile environment. Secondly 'Derivative Creation', in which users can freely imitate existing content. Due to these reasons, the creative entry barriers got lowered. More than 80% of TikTok users are working as content consumers and creators simultaneously.

However, the current Short-form video platform doesn't reflect this new culture and has new problems.

SERVICE CONCEPT

GEN-Z's alley experiences are cyclical; exploring, visiting, and sharing. However, the services they use do not fully reflect this flow of experience. We suggest the BRAND NEW WAY FOR GEN-Z’S ALLEYWAY EXPERIENCE

ONBOARDING

NFT Stikcer

Original content creators put stickers that express their characteristics on the content. Follow-Up content, which imitates the original content adds its sticker to the original. If the Follow-Up content is imitated again, it becomes derived (Pop-Up) content in recognition of the difference from the original!

Save Content Stickers in Folders
Follow-Up Saved Sticker Contents!
Or Just Follow-Up immidately
Add Sticker to Express your new context & creativity of your Follow-Up contents!

Contents Map

There are two types of Contents Map UI for exploration: flat mode suitable for image viewing and a 3D mode where you can see the hierarchical structure at a glance.

FLAT MODE

In Flat mode, the original content is located in the upper left corner, and the following-up content is listed on the x-axis and pop-up content on the y-axis.

SWITCH!

3D MODE

In 3D mode, pop-up content is connected under the original content at the top, and the thickness of the line connecting it indicates its importance. You can Zoon In&Out to check the information of contents.

Nock Nock is very short(4days) project to become friends with a product designer.

BACKGROUND

New Contact
Long-Awaited Package
Pleasant News

The definition of abundance is that there is plenty(NOCKNOCKHAM in Korean). When people receive unexpected good news, their hearts are soaked with emotion and overflowing with plenty. We designed Nock Nock , a gift box that can be placed at a personal desk to feel this moment in the office.

The visual motif of the product stems from the excitement of the notification when good news arrives on the messenger.

USEAGE

Usually Nock Nock is a light on the desk.t
Put the gift inside Nock Nock, turn the lid over, and put the letter in.
The surprise gift in Nock Nock will make your heart rich!

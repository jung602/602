2021 Membership Emergence Project is an exhibition of stories which shines light on the thoughts of members of Samsung Design Membership. In MEP , the members of Samsung Design Membership not only discover the other side of an object, but also explores on the true value they carry. In the process of exploration, the light shines on the thoughts and thoughts hold the light. We hope that the thoughts holding the light exhibited in the 2021 MEP will bloom as another light for those who look at us.

ABOUT OBJECTS

Through careful observation, we often discover the unseen side of objects. It is like discovering that an object which we once viewed to be one-dimensional 'line', actually had two-dimensional 'sides' depending on the point of view.
When light-filled thoughts are projected onto objects, they expand into three-dimension and bloom into a new dimension.

EXHIBITION SITE

As you pass through each zone representing the three keywords, Find, Change, and Extend, of Vivify(object) exhibition, you can see the process and result of how 9 teams including the branding team, captures and extends light into and of an object.

FIND & CHANGE

The Find(Object) zone, contains the process of deriving the interests of the 8 teams and their thoughts. It is a space that shows the process of discovering an object that looked simply as a one-dimensional 'line', actually had two-dimensional 'sides', depending on the point of view.

EXTEND

This is a passage through which space is transformed from 2D to 3D. Thoughts filled with light are projected onto objects, and extension to three dimension takes place and blooms into a new dimension. Just before facing the final result of the 8 teams, we meet the extension of (Object).

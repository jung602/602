The MOL

PROJECT TYPE
Team Project		 ROLE
UI & 3D Graphic		 CATEGORY
AR Interaction Design		 YEAR
2021

This project was conducted as an industry-academic cooperation project between Samsung Design Membership and Samsung Electronics MX division.
The MOL is a new music player that allows you to enjoy a music experience in the AR environment. Unlike traditional 2D environments, in a 3D AR environment, the possibility of a new way, Z-axis is suggested to the user. In this new environment, we presented the music player which is visible, touchable, and interactive so that users can enjoy synesthetic music, while also thinking about discovering new NUI and interactions applying Z-axis.

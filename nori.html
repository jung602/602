Traditional games allow children to develop basic social skills. They develop sociality by playing traditional games with friends, become creative by discovering new tools and using playground spaces, and learn self-control by following game rules.
For the next generation of children to overcome the loss of play rights due to COVID-19, we propose a new online playground where they can play traditional games with their friends, make their own game space and rules.

BACKGROUND

After COVID-19, children were deprived of play rights and stayed home alone. Their playtime was shortened, and their social score was dropped.

A Comprehensive Survey of Children in Seoul(2020)
A Comprehensive Survey of Children in Seoul(2020)

CONCEPT

Children develop sociality by playing games with friends, become creative by discovering new tools and using playground spaces, and learn self-control by following game rules. For the next generation, we propose a new online playground where they can communicate with their friends through video, build playground spaces, and use a simple block interface to make their own game rules.

UI CONCEPT

Play with Friends

At NORI Home, you can choose traditional games and make game rooms with friends!

After making a game room, You can wait for friends in the waiting room, and check your avatar.
You can choose unique avatars! Also, you can change costumes for your avatar.

Build a Playground

After preparing is done, build a playground for the game. With DRAG & DROP magnetic interaction, create 3D space easily.

Make a Rule

After building a playground, use the block interface to change traditional game rules and increase the fun of the game.

Divide roles with friends, Set the rules for roles.
Set up various rules, Simply change the rules with the block interface.
Set a mission that needs to be performed in the game in order for a specific role to win.
Control the power of the Avatar. It afferts the level of the touch interface during mission.
Give Avatar personality. Movement and speed will depend on it.

IT’S NORI TIME!

Let's get started! Enjoy the traditional game that we adjusted and communicate with friends through the touch interface and video!

Mission will give you the tense of the game.
You can experience the tension and joy of playing in the playground through touch interface motion during a mission.

EXPECTATION

NORI expects to become a new traditional game for the next generation, who is the metaverse native. Also, NORI h opes that the children can learn and enjoy through online playgrounds regardless of region or nationality.

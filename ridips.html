Ridips is a service that helps deaf people learn to communicate anywhere, with anyone by lip-reading.

BACKGROUND

Deaf people account for a large proportion of the disabled population. Only 3.9% of them communicate in sign language. Consequently, lip-reading and speaking skills are essential in their reality. However, there is no lip-reading learning service with verbal training system for the hearing impaired at the moment.

The results of the 2015 survey on the disabled, South Korea
Korea Federation of Disabled Persons

FIELD RESEARCH

Stakeholder Map and User Storyboard designed with the results of real-voice interviews with five hearing impaired people and two disability education experts.

STORYBOARD

When Seongju was young, parents called a speech therapist home and received therapy for five years.

Entering college and making new friends, He needed to correct lip reading and pronunciation

However, it felt ackward to learn with young children at the center.

In addition, He couldn’t afford the cost of treatment center since it was extremely high.

Then, He found Ridips that allows him to practice lip-reading and speaking!

He trained in the context of daily conversations through Ridips every day at home.

He felt proud of the feedback results on the app showing his improvement.

Through Ridips, He gained confidence in speaking and became more free to talk to new people.

SOLUTION

Using the ‘deep fake’ video morphing technology, Ridips can endlessly generate education contents, just right for any daily life situations.

UI CONCEPT

Learn Setting

Choose the voice of AI Assistant who will help your learning.

Stage

The stages are places in everyday life such as hospital and cafe. Clearing each stage, you can get a landmark. Let's expand the village by collecting new landmarks! You're AI assistant will help you!

Words and Sentences

Observe the shape of the mouth and pronounce the words and sentences out loud.

Actual Practice

Converse with an AI Learning Assistant and clear the mission!

Get badges and landmarks

As you learn step by step, you can get badges and landmarks.

Study Report

Get your training analysis and practice what you lack.
